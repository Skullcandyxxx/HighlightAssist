## Privacy Policy (Required for all stores)

**Privacy Policy for HighlightAssist**

Last Updated: November 9, 2025

**Data Collection**
HighlightAssist does NOT collect, store, or transmit any user data. Period.

**Permissions Explained**
- **activeTab:** Required to interact with the current tab when you click the extension icon
- **scripting:** Required to inject the highlighting tool into localhost pages
- **storage:** Used only to remember your on/off preference locally in your browser

**Host Permissions**
The extension only requests permission to run on:
- localhost
- 127.0.0.1
- *.local domains

It cannot and will not access any other websites.

**Third-Party Services**
None. The extension works completely offline and makes no external network requests.

**Data Sharing**
Since we don't collect any data, we have nothing to share.

**Children's Privacy**
This extension does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.
